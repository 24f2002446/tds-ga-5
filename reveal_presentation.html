Financial Math

Present the Net Present Value (NPV) formula and Discounted Cash Flow approximation:

$$\mathrm{NPV}=\sum_{t=0}^{T}\frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t}$$

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

$$\mathrm{WACC}=\frac{E}{V}R_e+\frac{D}{V}R_d(1-T_c)$$

Use these to justify discount rates in DCF sensitivity slide coming next.
